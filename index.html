Welcome to my website! My name is Haran Mouli. I am currently a senior at the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I major in mathematics. My interests include algebraic number theory and combinatorics. I am an active user on Math Stack Exchange.

I have been a part of the PROMYS program for several years, both in Boston and India. If you are a high school student or an undergraduate who is passionate about mathematics, I cannot recommend PROMYS enough. It's a wonderful place to learn math, both as a student and a counselor, and the community is inclusive, encouraging, and vibrant.

You can reach out to me at hmouli[at]wisc[dot]edu.
